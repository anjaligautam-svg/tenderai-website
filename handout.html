Platform Capabilities
Post Go-Live Contract Tracker
Reactive → Proactive
Tracks contract milestones after award, alerting officers before deadlines rather than after a breach. Structured timelines extracted from the awarded contract; AI flags upcoming deliverables and payment milestones proactively so no obligation is missed.
Pre-Bid Eligibility Self-Assessment
Met · Not Met · Borderline
Vendors self-screen against tender eligibility criteria before bidding — each criterion assessed as Met, Not Met, or Borderline. Reduces ineligible bids and disqualification disputes. Officers see the vendor-submitted evidence at evaluation time.
Live Bid Comparison
Auto-ranked + anomaly-flagged
Side-by-side comparative statement across all bids — TQ score, quoted amount, financial score and combined QCBS rank, downloadable in your template. AI flags abnormally low bids (e.g. −15.2% vs estimate) and recommends a course of action. Committee confirms; AI advises only.
Procurement Analytics
3 role-scoped lenses
Procurement Health (draft time, compliance adherence, evaluation cycle, savings vs estimate), Vendor Intelligence (win rate, repeat disqualification, savings per vendor), and AI Agent Performance (flag precision, draft acceptance, drift, confidence calibration) — each scoped to the viewer's role.

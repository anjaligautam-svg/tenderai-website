The reality today

Public procurement shouldn't carry this much risk.

A single tender can take months of file movement and approvals. Most delays and audit objections trace back to the same few causes, and officers are expected to get them right without structured guidance.

Paper-first, desk to desk

Documents and approvals scattered across physical files: slow to move, hard to trace, easy to misplace.

Audit objections

Missing documentation, the wrong cost basis or disproportionate eligibility: the recurring sources of CVC and CAG findings.

No structured guidance

Officers carry the process in their heads and fear getting it wrong, with little support and no safety net.

Questions

Answers for procurement teams.

Does TenderAI replace the officer or the approval process?

No. TenderAI is a co-pilot. It drafts, checks and tracks, but every compliance pointer is advisory and never hard-blocking, and all major signings and approvals happen offline. The decision always stays with the officer and the competent authority.

How does it keep confidential bid data protected?

Access is role-based: each role sees only what it is permitted to. Confidential quote amounts stay masked until they are formally revealed in a deliberate two-step action, and a Super Admin surveillance view records who accessed which confidential data, and when.

Which compliance norms does it align with?

The workflow follows Indian government norms (GFR 2017, CVC guidelines and the Manual for Procurement of Works, Goods and Services), and the consolidated CVC pre-check predicts the issues a reviewer is most likely to raise.

Can it generate documents in Hindi and Marathi?

Yes. Tender documents can be generated in English, Hindi or Marathi, with controls for logo and seal placement and for adding custom sections.

Is the portal accessible for all officers?

TenderAI is designed to meet India's GIGW 3.0 and UX4G guidance and WCAG 2.1 AA, including keyboard operation, visible focus, sufficient contrast and screen-reader support.

How does evaluation handle different methods?

Bid Evaluation supports L1, QCBS, LCS, QBS and FBS, with committee scoring, e-signatures, comparative statements and abnormally-low-bid detection, all under the same role-based confidentiality.
